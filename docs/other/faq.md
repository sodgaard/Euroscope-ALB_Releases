# FAQ

**Where do I download?** See [Download & Install](../download-install.md).  

**Which DLL should EuroScope load?** Load `ALB.dll`. Do not load `ALBCore.dll` directly.  

**What files should normally be in the plugin folder?** At minimum: `ALB.dll`, `ALBCore.dll`, and `alb-config.json`. Optional files such as `ALBLoader.ini` and `alb-config.default.json` may also be present.  

**How do I claim FMR?** See [Workflows](../user/workflows.md).

**How do I set EAT for an aircraft in hold?** See [Workflows](../user/workflows.md) and [Coordination of sequencing and EAT](../msgs/eat_detailing.md).
